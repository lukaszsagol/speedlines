About this game

Ã—

This is my remake of the retro polish game zuzel created by Piotr Kaminski.

When I was younger I used to play zuzel a lot and had a lot of fun playing it with my friends. Some time ago I decided that this simple game concept is ideal for a quick hack using not-so-new frontend technologies such as canvas. At the same time I was sure I will have a lot of fun doing that and hoped that you will have even more fun playing it.

I have some plans for future features, like online play, tournament mode and stuff like that.

Now just call your friends, get some beers and show your skills.

If you want to contact me I am @zgryw on Twitter or just drop me a line at lukasz@sagol.pl.

Used: Apple Keyboard CSS, Fragments of FlatUI, flatuicolors.com

How to play

Ã—

In-game controls are based on one key per player. The only way you can impact the path of your bike is pressing your key to make the bike turn left.

Remember not to hold the key too long at the end of the bend!
